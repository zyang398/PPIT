General Project Selection Material Construction Performance Standards/References

The types of HMA applied can be dense graded, open graded, stone matrix mixes, with or without high percentage of polymer-modified binder. This section focuses on dense graded layer and do not address other mix types, such as open-graded mixes. These materials and their construction requirements are described in Sections 400, 424, 824, and 828 of the Standard Specifications, 2013 Edition, in Special Provisions 400 and 828 issued Feb. 24, 2006, and in Special Provisions 424 and 824 issued Mar. 6, 2006. According to GDOT’s Asphalt Pavement Selection Guidelines, mix types are distinguished according to nominal maximum size of the aggregate in millimeters, i.e. 9.5 relates to an aggregate size of 9.5 millimeters, or 3/8 inch.
