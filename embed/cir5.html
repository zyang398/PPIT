General Project Selection Material Construction Performance Standards/References

Wet Weather Condition

Wet weather conditions would limit CIR since

• Rainy weather interrupts construction work, which requires moving large, slow equipment units on and off the project. Parking large equipment during construction is an issue because of the size and very slow speed of the equipment.

• Possible performance issues exist if rainy weather sets in before work is complete.

• Wet, cool weather delays the use of emulsion-based CIR and lengthens the curing time.
