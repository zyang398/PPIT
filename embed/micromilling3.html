General Project Selection Material Construction Performance Standards/References

Approved materials for GDOT are located on GDOT’s Qualified Products List (QPL) and defined in GDOT specification Section 400. Note that besides the material, the quality of the micro-milled pavement surface texture is critical for the performance of micromilling & thin overlay.   GDOT Specification Section 432, “Mill Asphalt Concrete Pavement,” (GDOT 2005) is used for micromilling.
