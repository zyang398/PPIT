Loose or Excess Stone

Loose stone from a chip seal operation has numerous negative consequences:

  1. Reduces skid resistance

  2. Can lead to windshield damage

  3. Can obscure striping

  4. Dislodges stone that would not otherwise be disturbed

  5. Contributes to negative public perception of chip seals

Loose stone can be caused by improper type or grade of bituminous material, paving when temperature conditions are not proper, improper or insufficient rolling, excessive application of stone, failure to adequately sweep the finished surface, or a combination of any of these factors.

Bleeding
