Ultra-Thin Bonded Wearing Course (UTBWC), also known as NovaChpTM, is designed for use as a high performance surface treatment over structurally sound asphalt or concrete pavements. UTBWC consists of a heavy spray application of polymer modified asphalt emulsion membrane followed immediately by an ultra-thin gap-graded hot mix asphalt (HMA). Both applications are quick completed in one pass by a specially designed paving machine. Once the hot mixed asphalt is placed, a 10-ton steel wheeled vibratory roller is used for compaction. The area can be opened to regular traffic usually within 15 minutes after compaction is completed.

The benefits of UTBWC include:

• Superior bonding of the new surface to the existing surface.

• Impermeable membrane helps seal out water.

• Reduces noise.

• Improves skid resistance.

• Maintains overhead clearances, curbs and drainage profiles.

• Provides fast construction/return to traffic – lowers delay costs.

• Minimizes user inconvenience.
