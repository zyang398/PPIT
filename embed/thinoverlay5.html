General Project Selection Material Construction Performance Standards/References

Pavement condition

Thin overlay is intended to replace the worn-out surface layer (less than 1.5”) while the underlying layer is still structurally sound. A pavement with a PACES rating less or equal to 70 with moderate cracking (e.g., levels 1, 2, and 3 load cracking and levels 1 and 2 block cracking) and limited rutting (< 1/2”) would be most appropriate, but a pavement with localized rutting or load cracking levels 3 and 4 would also be a suitable candidate with proper deep patching. Pavements with severe rutting or structure damage should not be considered.
