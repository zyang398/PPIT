Joint With Spall

Definition:

The deterioration of concrete at the joint usually results in the breaking or flaking off of the concrete at the joint; as they worsen raveling and pop-outs occur. Figure 12 shows examples of joints with spalls. The number or severity of spalls is not accounted for, just the total number of joints with spalls; therefore, if a joint has two or more spalls a single entry is made showing one spalled joint. According to the specifications, a spall must be at least 1.5" x 6" in area. We will use this minimum size to define the minimum spall size.
